01

Colors

Our palette is built around a bold purple core with a dark navy anchor. Supplemental colors add energy and are used for data visualization, highlights, and illustrations.

Core colors

Purple 600 #4E50D1
Black #051B2C

Supplemental colors

Supplemental colors should be used sparingly and should not replace core or neutral colors. They may be applied if diversity or flexibility in color is needed such as in a complex chart, diagram, or illustration.

Purple 500 #7274DA
Blue 400 #4DA6EF
Cyan 300 #31E1DE
Yellow 300 #FFC072

Neutral colors should primarily be used for text and backgrounds.
